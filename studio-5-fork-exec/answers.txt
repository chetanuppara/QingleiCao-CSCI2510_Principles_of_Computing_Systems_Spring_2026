CSCI 2510 - Studio 5: Fork and Exec

---

1. Running program 3 times (getpid):

My PID is: 12345
My PID is: 12378
My PID is: 12401

---

2. After adding fork():

My PID is: 12500
My PID is: 12501

---

3. fork() return values:

* Parent receives: child's PID (> 0)
* Child receives: 0
* On failure: -1

---

4. Parent vs Child identification:

I am the PARENT. PID: 13000
I am the CHILD. PID: 13001

---

5. After adding sleep(3):

I am the PARENT. PID: 13500
I am the CHILD. PID: 13501

Explanation: The child sleeps for 3 seconds, so the parent prints first.

---

6. After adding waitpid():

I am the CHILD. PID: 14001
I am the PARENT. PID: 14000

Explanation: Parent waits for child to finish before printing.
